About CYBER

CYBER is a digital and factor-based currency designed to be a reliable means of payment.

We created CYBER due to the multiple weaknesses of common currencies such as high inflations, economic vulnerabilities, a centralization and a lack of trust. The mission behind CYBER is to create a trustworthy, value-stable, global and decentralized currency based on hundreds of factors to get as close as possible to real prices. CYBER is not a speculative asset. It is intended to be a reliable means of payment.

With the development of the Metaverse, another business field is opened up. CYBER is supposed to be used in real life as well as in the Metaverse.
